Overview

Skill Exchange is an online service connecting people to each other based on skills that are in demand. Essentially, it is the basic give & take principle: I do something for you, and you do something for me.

Skill Exchange is a progressive new technology. Though there are other websites such as LinkedIn, Craigslist, and Skillshare, we are different from them in a fundamental way: we do not use currency. Instead, we simply provide a service to trade skills and do work for each other.

We make what is basically volunteering laughably easy. All you have to do is make a request, agree to some terms (compensation, deadlines), and do the work! We will manage the rest.

We are dedicated to ensuring our customers' convenience and security. That is why we monitor every profile and take immediate action if anything is reported. We have a fully staffed legal and security team. In short, we have your back.
